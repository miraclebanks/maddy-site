about the baker

Hi, I'm Maddy.

I started baking out of my kitchen because I wanted to make cakes that taste like they came from someone who actually likes you. No giant industrial bakery vibes. No mystery frosting. Just real butter, real fruit, and a little bit of madness.

Every cake is made to order, decorated by hand, and built around whatever you're celebrating — birthdays, weddings, Tuesdays, breakups, you name it.

  • 🌿 small batch & seasonal
  • 🫐 real ingredients, no shortcuts
  • 💌 custom designs welcome
